Privacy

How this site handles your data. The short version: it collects very little and shares nothing.

What this site collects

This site does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. There is no advertising, no pixel tracking, and no behavioural profiling.

If you use the contact form, the information you submit (name, email address, enquiry type, and message) is processed by Formspree, a third-party form handling service based in the United States. Formspree acts as a data processor on my behalf. Your submission is forwarded to me by email and stored by Formspree in accordance with their privacy policy. I use this data solely to respond to your enquiry.

Google Fonts

This site uses Google Fonts to display typefaces. When you load a page, your browser makes a request to Google's servers, which means Google receives your IP address. Google states that it does not use this data for advertising or to create user profiles. For full details, see Google's privacy policy.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data I hold about you. If you've contacted me through the form and would like your data removed, email julie@juliehendry.com and I will action the request promptly.

Data controller

Julie Hendry, contactable at julie@juliehendry.com. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's privacy practices are detailed in their privacy statement.
