Why 75% of Transformations Fail

It's never the technology. The tech always works eventually. It's these six psychological barriers:

01

Trust Deficit

People don't trust leadership's motives or competence. They've seen "transformations" before.

02

Skill Anxiety

Fear of being left behind, made redundant, or exposed as not keeping up.

03

Leadership Invisibility

Executives announce change then disappear. They don't "go see" what's actually happening.

04

Decision Paralysis

Wrong decisions at wrong levels. Too slow, too fast, or made by people too far from reality.

05

Value Confusion

Nobody agrees what success looks like. Metrics measure activity, not outcomes.

06

Governance Theatre

Process over outcomes. Rituals performed, boxes ticked, nothing actually changes.
